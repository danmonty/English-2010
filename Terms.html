The Adventurer

The Adventuring Dictionary

Term Definition
Class The specific training and skills that the characters have; examples of classes: Barbarian, Bard, Cleric, Fighter, Ranger, Sorccer, and Wizard
d20 A dice that has 20 sides with numbers 1-20; used for any abililty checks, attack rolls, or saving throws
DM Stands for Dungeon Master; The Narrator of the Quest; Plays as all NPC; aka Game Master (GM)
Homebrew A quest or world which the DM makes up
NPC Stands for Non-Player Characters; All extra characters in the story but played by the DM
Race The main races players can choose to be are Elves, Dwarfs, Gnomes, Orcs, and Humans; each race gives unique skills and abilities
