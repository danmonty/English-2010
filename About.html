All About The Adventurer

Interested in learning more about Dungeons and Dragons? Curious to see if you have what it takes to become a fearless adventurer? Are you a seasoned dungeon delver who wants some hints or tricks to create the best character? Well you have come to the right place. This site is dedicated to discussing anything D&D.

My name is Danny Montel. I started playing D&D around the summer of 2016. I have played homebrew and prebuilt quests; I have even DM a few times, and I love D&D. When I first started playing, I was lost and confused. My first character was a wizard; choosing a magic class for the first time player is a giant mistake. I want to help new players not make the same mistakes. I also want to help some of the more experienced players learn how to continue to be good players.

Face your fears, whether it be an evil necromancer or an adult red dragon. I hope that you continue to stay tuned and keep on adventuring!
